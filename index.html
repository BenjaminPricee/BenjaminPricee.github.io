  • Project Deception

    Project Deception is the name I have decided to release games under throughout my college experience. It is a horror-focused, solo-developer project based in Ireland.

    Project Deception has helped me expand my network into the Games Community and even helped me build a small following. I have been met with nothing but support and hope to continue to make you scream.

  • Night Nurse

    Night Nurse was the first game released by Project Deception. The game takes place in Haughton Psychiatric Hospital. You play as a nurse during her first lock-up shift. The doctor has left a set of instructions: deliver the medpacks, go home. Easy, right? You'll soon find that the faulty lights and power are not your biggest problem...
